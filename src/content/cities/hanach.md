## tagline
Capital of the Hanacene Empire and seat of Princess Chalexis.

## overview
Hanach stands at the heart of the Thyalix plain — the capital that Velkharan, the half-silver-dragon sorcerer who founded the Second Empire nearly four centuries ago, established as the seat of his reconquest. A city of nearly a hundred thousand souls, its skyline is crowned by the **Argent Spire**, an aerie of the dragons millenia past. The city remains the largest settlement in the empire and the center of imperial administration, trade, and military command.

## politics
Princess Chalexis rules from the imperial palace, supported by the Karindel family — earls of Thyalix and the Empire's chancellors, with Hector Karindel serving as her closest advisor. House Aldaine holds the eastern approaches, but are lower in the imperial pecking order than their northern rivals House Solentis and the wealthy House Karathex. The city's patrician class, led by guild-masters and port authorities, broadly support Chalexis for the stability of trade.

## skyknight-presence
The capital is home to the Imperial Skyknights, wyvern-mounted warriors of great renown. It is here that the wyverns are bred and raised into steeds that patrol the skies, protecting the imperial core from aerial attacks. They are also an unsubtle reminder that wyverns will only bond with riders carrying some trace of draconic blood, a privilege the empire's ruling lineages have kept very close.

## underworld
Beneath Hanach's clean civic face lies a warren of old tunnels repurposed as smuggling routes, cult gathering places, and the meeting grounds of spies and agents of various powers.
