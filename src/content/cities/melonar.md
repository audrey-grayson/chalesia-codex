## tagline
Seat of House Aldaine — the dragon-blooded eastern lords who anchor the Chalexis faction.

## overview
Melonar is built economically on the agrarian lowlands - the city is a port of egress for the grain produced inland, trading for fine goods from the provinces, Acenia, and the Lanian elves to the south. House Aldaine has held Aurem for generations on the strength of an ancestral silver-dragon line, reliably producing more sorcerers than the other houses. The city itself is prosperous but quiet, without a large military presence.

## dragon-blood
The silver-dragon sorcery runs strong in House Aldaine: every generation seems to produce at least one capable sorcerer. Lord Maverick Aldaine holds the seat; Lady Salix, his wife, is Tremaine-born and carries the red-dragon blood of that house. It is said that Calypso, the scion of the house, wields *both* powers.
