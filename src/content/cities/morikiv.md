## tagline
A small inland settlement in northern Chilix — Iaryx-aligned by province, otherwise unremarked.

## overview
Morikiv is a small village of roughly three thousand in the foothills of the western mountains. The town is part of Iaryx's domain by virtue of Chilix's faction allegiance, but in practice it sits far enough from the war's front lines to be left mostly to its own affairs.

Further lore for this settlement is yet to be written.
