## tagline
A small inland settlement in northern Chilix — Iaryx-aligned by province, otherwise unremarked.

## overview
Morikiv is a small village of roughly four thousand souls in the northern reaches of Chilix province, west of Kleover. The town is administratively part of Iaryx's domain by virtue of Chilix's faction allegiance, but in practice it sits far enough from the war's front lines to be left mostly to its own affairs.

Further lore for this settlement is yet to be written.
