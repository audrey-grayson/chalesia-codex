## tagline
King Halkir's seat — an ancient trading city with deep claims to imperial legitimacy.

## overview
Pelath is among the oldest cities in the empire — it was founded during the Fractured Kingdoms era, and King Halkir claims unbroken lineage back to Seneca, founder of the First Empire. The city is a natural trading hub, sitting to the north along the western shore of the Bay of Diamonds. 

## legitimacy
Halkir's claim rests on lineage, not military power or magic. His genealogists have produced documentation — disputed by Chalexis's court but not definitively disproved — tracing his family to Seneca's younger son. Many of the Empress' allies view this as politically convenient revisionism.
