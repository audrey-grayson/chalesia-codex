## tagline
The Iron City — second largest in the empire, seat of the Iventhyr barons and loyal to General Iaryx.

## overview
Zorlatra sits at the confluence of two rivers in Enester province. With a population of forty-two thousand it is the second city of the empire, yet it holds only a barony — a slight that the Iventhyr line have nursed for generations.

When Imperator Iaryx declared for reform, Baron Iventhyr was the first of the grand lords to commit fully. Zorlatra's industry and veterans form the backbone of the Iaryx war effort.
