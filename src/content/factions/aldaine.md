## tagline
The Silver Sorcerers — dragon-blooded lords of Aurem, anchor of the Chalexis faction.

## overview
House Aldaine carries silver-dragon blood in its line, some say inherited directly from the royal family. They claim their family's sorcerers have bred true for generations, and it's known that the Aldaines produce the most likely sorcerous offspring of the nobility. In the current generation the gifts are reportedly very strong, thanks to the arranged union of Lord Maverick Aldaine to Salix of house Tremaine, a red dragon sorcerer.
They have backed Chalexis without hesitation.

## banner-men
House Aldaine commands several lesser houses in Aurem and the adjacent province of Ilthyrion (House Mordell). The current Lady Aldaine, Salix, is Tremaine-born, but that alliance has become politically awkward since the war began: Tremaine declared for Chalexis alongside the Aldaines, but Iaryx's armies have since overrun the Tremaine seat in Irdagar, leaving the Aldaines' principal allies titled but without land or incomes.
