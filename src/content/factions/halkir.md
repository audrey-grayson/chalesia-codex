## tagline
The Merchant Lord — trader-king of Pelath, claiming descent from the First Empire's founder.

## overview
Lord Halkir presents himself as the restoration candidate — a return to the values of Seneca, the First Empire's founder, from whom he claims unbroken descent. His faction is the smallest militarily, but the richest. He has cultivated the Free Cities of the eastern straits and used their merchant networks to fund mercenaries, buy intelligence, and quietly build goodwill in cities that Chalexis and Iaryx have been taxing to exhaustion.
