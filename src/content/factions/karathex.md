## tagline
Lords of Karathex Province — a wartime Chalexis ally in the eastern interior.

## overview
House Karathex governs the province that shares their name, a mid-sized eastern territory. They declared for Chalexis early in the war, apparently as a matter of conviction rather than calculation — the current lord has a reputation for blunt loyalty that has not always served him well politically. Their province has not yet seen direct military action, though this may change.
