## tagline
Lords of Vinat — a contested march family caught between advancing armies.

## overview
House Sentaire holds Vinat County, one of the most actively contested regions of the civil war. The province carries the "wartime" designation in imperial records alongside three settlements, meaning the lord is either a hero of the resistance or a collaborator — depending entirely on which army you ask. The Sentaires have reportedly changed their stated allegiance twice already.
