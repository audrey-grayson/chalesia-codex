## tagline
The Red Sorcerers — Barons of Irdagar, dragon-blooded carriers of chromatic fire; declared for Chalexis, dispossessed by Iaryx.

## overview
House Tremaine carries red-dragon blood, which they proudly display on their sigil. The family traces its bloodline back over eight hundred years, and has maintained respectability through consistent imperial service. Lady Salix Aldaine, fourth child of the current Baron, is Tremaine-born, giving her firstborn daughter Calypso an unparalleled grasp of sorcery through the union of two draconic bloodlines.

## civil-war-fortunes
Tremaine declared for Princess Chalexis at the opening of the civil war, but the decision proved costly. Their ancestral fiefdom of Irdagar lies on the western side of the empire, exposed to Iaryx's advance, and his armies overran the province in the war's early years. The Tremaine line remains nobility by title and bloodline — and their sorcerers still serve in Chalexis's councils and on her battlefields — but they no longer hold their lands.
