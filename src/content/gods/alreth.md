## tagline
The Sower — most-prayed-to of the Hanacene pantheon.

## description
Known as the Sower, Alreth governs the harvest and is the most commonly prayed to of all the gods — attracting his favor is seen as critical for any agricultural settlement. His children with Myrai are a vast array of minor harvest gods. Simple and unassuming in depictions, soldiers defending their homes also pray to Alreth to return safely to their families.

## worshippers
Farmers, soldiers fighting defensively, families, village communities.
