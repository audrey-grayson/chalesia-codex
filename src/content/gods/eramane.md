## tagline
The Veiled One — genderless god of knowledge, secrets, and shadow.

## description
Genderless, depicted swathed in shadows with only a single eye visible. Priests often go fully covered and veiled. Patron of wizards, sages, and spies, Eramane governs the transfer, hoarding, and discovery of all knowledge. Their myths include an Odin-like trade in which they gave away the secret of their own identity in exchange for virtual omniscience. Associated with the Plane of Shadow.

## worshippers
Wizards, scholars, sages, spies, archivists, and intelligence networks.
