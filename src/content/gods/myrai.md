## tagline
The Greenmother — goddess of the untamed world.

## description
Myrai governs the untamed wilderness, the savage sea, and the fertility and growth of all living things. Everchanging and mercurial, she is nurturing and kind in summer, but cold and hateful in winter. She is usually depicted as a mother deity, though her sex is fluid — she has taken children with several other gods of differing genders.

## worshippers
Farmers, druids, hunters, coastal communities, and anyone who depends on the natural world.
