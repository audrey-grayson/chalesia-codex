## tagline
The Inevitable — god of death and fate, worshipped out of fear.

## description
Widely feared and shunned, even by the other gods. He is not opposed to undeath — necromancer cults worship him openly. Prayer to Soranus is like protection money: he receives worship from people trying to avoid death — travelers, soldiers, the seriously ill. His rivalry with Syltea is friendly: both hold sway over patterns and fate, but she alone understands the darkness of night and the severing of connections. The shadowy cults of the Soranic Mysteries claim that one day all the other gods will die, leaving only Soranus remaining.

## worshippers
Necromancers, the dying, soldiers, travelers, those who fear death most.
