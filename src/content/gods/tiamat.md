## tagline
The Dragon Queen — progenitor of chromatic dragons, a dark mirror of Calitax.

## description
A premodern primordial — one of the Old Gods who predated the current pantheon. Tiamat is the progenitor of chromatic dragons and a dark mirror of Calitax: legend says she copied the Queen of Conquest's designs to build a rival army of destruction. A Chaotic Evil god of chaos and destruction, her desire is to return the world to its elemental essence so that she may build a new Material Plane from the ashes. Her worshippers are rare and mostly hidden.

## worshippers
Chromatic dragon cults, apocalyptic sects, chromatic dragons themselves.
